If you know certain characters, they can be inserted in place of a number. '*' or '?' matches any character.

For example, if you know the clue is 4, 4 and you know the second letter of the second word is q, you can enter 4,?q2 which matches damp squib

This tool can be used to find common English phrases that match a particular given pattern. This is useful for people like me who haven't come across many of the phrases used in cryptic (and regular?) crosswords.
